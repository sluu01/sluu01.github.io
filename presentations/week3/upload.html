Minimalism at its Finest

Living in a Minimal World

Minimalism is the art of creating a website that is simple, pleasing, easy on the eye, and free of clutter. It should look clean, professional, yet catches people's attention. Things that should be considered in a minimalist website:

  • Colours
  • Fonts
  • Graphics
  • No more than two typefaces per webpage
  • Dual colour schemes
  • Only lines for shapes
